About Conference

All India IEEE Computer Society Student & Young Professional Congress unites the members and representatives of various

IEEE student branches across India, as well as numerous enterprising pupils too. It is a chance for everyone to engage in

thought-provoking discussions on technological stacks, participate in industrial application tracks and also learn the importance of IEEE in industrial and academic level.

This event marks the spirit of cognition and research.

  • Panel Discussions
  • Technological Seminars
  • Hands-on Experience
  • Educative Getaway
